MediLink is an innovative Android application designed to reduce the need for recurring physical visits to clinics or doctors by enabling seamless digital communication between healthcare providers and patients. The app allows doctors to send prescriptions and medical reports directly to their patients, while patients can provide health updates and feedback without the need for an in-person visit.
